# 1. O que é o Verb To Be?

O **verb to be** significa **ser** ou **estar**.

Ele é usado para:
- Identidade → I am a teacher.
- Estado → She is tired.
- Localização → They are at home.
- Profissão → He is a doctor.

---

# 2. PRESENTE (Present)

## Forma afirmativa

| Sujeito | Forma | Pronúncia (brasileirês) |
|----------|--------|--------------------------|
| I | am | ãm |
| You | are | ar |
| He | is | iz |
| She | is | iz |
| It | is | iz |
| We | are | ar |
| They | are | ar |

---

## Contrações (forma curta)

| Forma completa | Contração | Pronúncia |
|----------------|------------|------------|
| I am | I’m | áim |
| You are | You’re | iór |
| He is | He’s | ríiz |
| She is | She’s | xíiz |
| It is | It’s | its |
| We are | We’re | uír |
| They are | They’re | dér |

---

## Exemplos

- I am happy. (ai ãm répi)
- I’m happy. (áim répi)

- She is tired. (xi iz táierd)
- She’s tired. (xíiz táierd)

- They are ready. (dêi ar rédi)
- They’re ready. (dér rédi)

---

## Negativa

| Forma completa | Contração | Pronúncia |
|----------------|------------|------------|
| am not | (não tem contração comum) | ãm not |
| is not | isn’t | íznt |
| are not | aren’t | árnt |

Exemplos:
- She isn’t here. (xi íznt rír)
- They aren’t ready. (dêi árnt rédi)

---

## Pergunta

Am/Is/Are + sujeito

- Are you ok? (ar iú ôkei)
- Is she ready? (iz xi rédi)

---

# 3. PASSADO (Past)

## Forma afirmativa

| Sujeito | Forma | Pronúncia |
|----------|--------|------------|
| I | was | uóz |
| You | were | uâr |
| He | was | uóz |
| She | was | uóz |
| It | was | uóz |
| We | were | uâr |
| They | were | uâr |

---

## Contrações negativas

| Forma completa | Contração | Pronúncia |
|----------------|------------|------------|
| was not | wasn’t | uóznt |
| were not | weren’t | uârnt |

Exemplos:
- I was tired. (ai uóz táierd)
- I wasn’t tired. (ai uóznt táierd)

- They were late. (dêi uâr lêit)
- They weren’t late. (dêi uârnt lêit)

---

## Pergunta

Was/Were + sujeito

- Was she home? (uóz xi rôm)
- Were they ready? (uâr dêi rédi)

---

# 4. FUTURO (Future)

No futuro usamos:

WILL BE

## Forma afirmativa

| Forma | Pronúncia |
|--------|------------|
| will be | uíl bí |

Ex:
- I will be there. (ai uíl bí dér)
- She will be a doctor. (xi uíl bí a dóktor)

---

## Contração

| Forma completa | Contração | Pronúncia |
|----------------|------------|------------|
| I will | I’ll | áil |
| You will | You’ll | iúl |
| He will | He’ll | ríul |
| She will | She’ll | xíul |
| We will | We’ll | uíl |
| They will | They’ll | déul |
| will not | won’t | uôunt |

Exemplos:
- I’ll be there. (áil bí dér)
- She’ll be happy. (xíul bí répi)
- I won’t be late. (ai uôunt bí lêit)

---

# 5. RESUMO GERAL

| Tempo | Forma |
|--------|--------|
| Presente | am / is / are |
| Passado | was / were |
| Futuro | will be |

## Leitura

Maria is happy today. Yesterday she was tired. Tomorrow she will be relaxed.

Responda:

1. Is Maria happy today? Yes
2. Was she tired yesterday? Yes
